Hello!

We are a group of year 12 students at Marist College who care greatly about sustainability of the environment.

Did you know, 79% of plastic waste ends up in landfills, dumps, and oceans?

To help address this, we have come up with a business idea, creating edible cutlery that is sustainable for the environment and reduces plastic waste.





Our First Batch



Our team here at EcoEats produced homemade edible cutlery and packaging for our 1st market day ever.

It was a success! We sold out of most of our edible cutlery packs.

At our 2nd market day, the nationwide famous ACTS party leader, David Seymour, took a picture with us and bought an edible cutlery pack to support our business mission to reduce plastic waste in the environment.
